The BurgerShaack

Established in 1978 in the small town of Eastleigh , Edenvale. The Burger Shaack is a family-ran business which provides the best burger cuisine around. it was created by our great grandfather Felix Khoza, orignially as source of extra income but became a symbol in the neighbourhood. Since then the restuarant and its secrets have passed down from one generation to the next, feeding many families along the way. The Burger Shaack offers a warm family like experience, we see you as family not to just a customer. This unique selling point has made The Burger Shaack successfull and relevant over the changing times. Judge us by how we treat you, not only on how the food tastes.
